# Privacy Policy for Hyu Ramadhan Planner

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Hyu Ramadhan Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location data (except for prayer time calculations, which are processed locally on your device)

### 2. App Usage Data
The App stores the following data **locally on your device**:
- **Ibadah Tracking Data**: Your daily prayer completions, fasting records, Quran reading progress, charity activities, and other worship activities you choose to track
- **Gamification Data**: XP points, level progress, unlocked badges, and achievement records
- **App Preferences**: Your Ramadhan configuration settings (start date, target khatam count), theme preferences, and notification settings
- **Daily Challenges**: Your challenge completion status and progress

### 3. Technical Data
- **Device Information**: Basic device information required for the App to function properly (screen size, OS version for compatibility)
- **Crash Logs**: Anonymous error reports to help us improve the App (if you choose to share them)

## How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely for:
1. **Providing Core Functionality**: To track your ibadah progress, calculate prayer times, and display your achievements
2. **Gamification Features**: To award XP, track levels, and unlock badges based on your activities
3. **Personalization**: To remember your preferences and provide a customized experience
4. **App Improvement**: To fix bugs and improve user experience (anonymous crash reports only)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your personal worship data is stored **locally on your device** using Hive database and SharedPreferences
- **No data is transmitted to our servers** or stored in the cloud
- Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export it

### Data Security
- We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data on your device
- Data is stored in the app's private storage area, inaccessible to other apps
- We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated for optimal security

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### We Do NOT:
- Sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties
- Share your worship data with any external services
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track you across other apps or websites

### Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party libraries for functionality:
- **Geolocator**: For calculating prayer times based on your device's location (processed locally)
- **Confetti**: For celebration animations
- **Flutter Animate**: For UI animations

These libraries do not collect or transmit your personal data.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
You have full control over your data:
- **View**: All your data is visible within the App interface
- **Modify**: You can update or correct your tracking data at any time
- **Delete**: You can clear app data through your device settings or uninstall the App
- **Export**: You can export your data using the CSV export feature (if available)

### Data Retention
- Your data is retained on your device until you delete the App or clear app data
- Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect identifiable information, parents can allow their children to use the App with confidence.

## Prayer Times and Location

### Location Permission
- The App may request location permission to calculate accurate prayer times
- Location data is processed **locally on your device** and is **never transmitted** to our servers
- You can deny location permission and manually set your location in the App settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
